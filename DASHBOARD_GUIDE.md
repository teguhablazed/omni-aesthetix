# Omni AesthetiX - Quick Start Guide

Welcome to your luxury clinic management system. This guide covers the core AI-driven workflows.

## 1. Point of Sale (POS) & Automated Inventory
To ensure your inventory levels stay accurate:
- Navigate to the **POS** page.
- Select the active **Patient**.
- Add treatments to the cart.
- Click **Complete Order**.
- **Result**: The system automatically deducts stock from your inventory based on the treatment's recipe (e.g., Botox vials, Filler units).

## 2. Executive Dashboard & Analytics
Monitor your clinic's pulse in real-time:
- Check the **Overview Dashboard** for live revenue and patient growth stats.
- **Revenue Trend**: Visualizes your weekly performance.
- **Stock Alerts**: High-visibility "Red Alert" widgets notify you immediately if supplies are low.
- **AI Insights**: Click "Generate Insights" for strategic recommendations like promo ideas.

## 3. AI Clinical Progress (Vision)
Analyze and document patient results:
- Go to the **CRM** and select a patient.
- Navigate to the **Clinical Analysis** tab.
- Select two photos (Before & After).
- Click **Run AI Analysis**.
- **Result**: Qwen2-VL generates a professional clinical report comparing texture, pigmentation, and brightness.

## 4. Engagement Center
Maintain patient loyalty with AI:
- The **Engagement Center** shows patients due for a follow-up.
- Click the **Checkmark** on a message draft to edit the AI-generated luxurious outreach.
- Click **Send WhatsApp** to connect with patients instantly.
- Mark items as **Completed** once the outreach is done.
